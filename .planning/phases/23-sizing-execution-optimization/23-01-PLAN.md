<objective>
Recalibrate position sizing, switch stop-loss orders to maker fees, and reduce BTC OBI frequency.

Purpose: The forensic audit found that conviction_power=2.0 reduces mid-conviction positions to 25% of max (too small to overcome fees), stop-loss orders pay taker fees (50% premium), and OBI fires too frequently on BTC causing fee-negative trades. These three changes directly improve per-trade profitability.

Output: Updated configs (conviction_power, OBI cooldown), STOP_LIMIT stop-loss with configurable buffer, updated tests.
</objective>
